This app contains hundreds of gift ideas!

It takes into account the age, gender, and interests of the recipient so that you can find the perfect gift for anyone. There are also a variety of events and extras to choose from to cover all your gift-giving needs. There's even a "Random Gift" section if you don't want to filter by category.

About App development:

  • Developed an Android app that enables users to find gifts by category, by age, or randomly.
  • Utilized the SQLite database to store and handle data for the gift ideas and link them to Amazon product listings.

Skills:

Java · Android · Android Studio · SQLite
